THIS IS ABOUT US

Social Conclave is a unique national conference wherein the delegates from all over the country will assemble to examine the different social and financial concerns tormenting our nation. The aim of the conference is to discover doable arrangements for the issues which are implemented by Team Social Impact. Throughout the conference, numerous discussions will be presented, allowing delegates to learn about the perspectives of many notable experts.

The chief objective of Social Conclave is to entertain the delegates in a few rousing discourses and debate sessions, with the trust of changing the comfort of India for the superior. We'll take the delegates on a field trip to help them get to the bottom of the situation. We are optimistic that the conference would usher in a new era of change-makers in India.
