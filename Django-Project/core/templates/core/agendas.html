Super Core Conclave 2023

Wildlife and Ecosystem Protection

Our land of incredible biodiversity is facing a severe threat to its wildlife and ecosystems. Urbanization, poaching and improper waste disposal are some of the major forces driving the decline of several species of flora and fauna. We must develop effective measures for the protection and conservation of the environment to maintain the balance in the ecosystem. A sustainable approach to development would ensure that wildlife can coexist with humans.

Holistic Child Development

Underprivileged children are confronted with various challenges such as lack of education, malnutrition, limited access to healthcare and forced labor. Moreover, the extracurricular opportunities, and emotional development are often overlooked in favor of fulfilling more basic needs due to poverty. To ensure that children have a healthy and fulfilling childhood, we must formulate comprehensive child development programs that prioritize their social and emotional skills through extracurricular activities. Adults who were allowed to fully develop during their childhood can have a more positive impact on society.

Drug Rehabilitation & Recovery

India is facing a growing drug addiction problem, which is taking a toll on individuals, families, and society as a whole. Addiction can lead to a range of social, economic, and health-related problems that can have far-reaching impacts both in terms of the number and generations of people that it affects. It is important to develop effective drug rehabilitation and recovery programs that focus on prevention, treatment, and recovery. Such programs should include a range of interventions, such as counseling, medication-assisted therapy, and support groups, to help individuals overcome addiction and lead a healthy and productive life.

Reproductive Health and Rights

Reproductive health and rights are crucial for the well-being of women. However, many women in India lack access to reproductive health services and information, leading to unwanted pregnancies, unsafe abortions, and maternal mortality. It is essential to facilitate women with the access to comprehensive reproductive health services, family planning, maternal health care, and safe abortion services. These improvements will not only have a huge impact on women's reproductive health but also contribute to the overall development of the country by empowering women and promoting gender equality.
