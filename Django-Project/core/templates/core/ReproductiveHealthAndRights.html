About the Agenda

Reproductive health and rights are crucial for the well-being of women. However, many women in India lack access to reproductive health services and information, leading to unwanted pregnancies, unsafe abortions, and maternal mortality. It is essential to facilitate women with the access to comprehensive reproductive health services, family planning, maternal health care, and safe abortion services. These improvements will not only have a huge impact on women's reproductive health but also contribute to the overall development of the country by empowering women and promoting gender equality.
